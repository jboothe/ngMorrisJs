Ngmorrisjs

This AngularJS wrapper directive for MorrisJS Charts. MorrisJS Charts are some of the easiest charts to implement.

Why Another MorrisJS Angular Directive?

Although there are other wrappers out there, I needed a simple wrapper whereby ANY or ALL of the MorrisJS options could be passed. Point... not the first but one of the easiest to use, yet covers all the MorrisJS options.
